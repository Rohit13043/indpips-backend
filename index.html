FAQ

Questions, answered

How do payouts work?
Once funded you keep 80% of profits. You can request a payout after a set number of profitable days (≥0.25% each): 7 days for 1-Step, 5 for 2-Step, 4 for Instant. Paid to bank or UPI.
What are the drawdown rules?
1-Step & 2-Step: 4% daily and 6% trailing max drawdown on balance. Instant: a single 5% trailing drawdown on equity. All limits are clearly shown live on your dashboard.
Is news and weekend trading allowed?
News trading is allowed on all programs. Weekend holding is allowed on Instant accounts; 1-Step and 2-Step are weekday-only.
Which platform do I trade on?
MetaTrader 5 (MT5). Trade your own strategy — manual or EA within our fair-use rules.
How fast can I get funded?
Instant accounts are funded immediately. 1-Step and 2-Step have no minimum trading days, so you can pass as soon as you hit the target.
